About

World's First & Virtual NUTPAM 2020!! Bounding the theme 'ANYONE CAN CODE', Sri Sairam Institute of Technology is hosting a one-day Tech Fest virtually to bring out talent's among budding engineers. It becomes interesting when code meets fun and when it happens from your own placeholder!!!

Going Virtual

For the safety of the 1000+ people involved at the yearly event, NUTPAM 2020 has decided to move completely online for the 2020 season. We will still offer sponsor challenges, awesome prizes, fun games and more! See you on 29th October!

Grab The Infinites

REGISTER

Description

  • This technical event consists of 6 different programs and where each of the programs are interlinked to each other.
  • Initially the coder starts with a problem and if solves a problem then he will get a string output along with the next program he/she has to solve.
  • The clue or the solutions from the initial program is given as a test/custom input by the user to next program to get the next clue and next program he has to solve.So, this process continues until he solves the final program (6th one) and got the very last result revealed to the moderators.
  • The fastest hand wins the game.
  • All the coders must have "HackerRank "account and all the languages in HackerRank are allowed.
  • Individual participation.

F.A.Q

What is a Nutpam 2020?
It is a one day symposium conducted by the students of Computer Science and Engineering Department of Sri Sairam Institute of Technology.

Who can come?
Nutpam 2020 is open to any enrolled undergraduate or graduate student.

When is the event? How does registration work?
The event is on October 29, 2020 and the Registration will happen through Google Forms.
